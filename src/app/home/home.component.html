Features

I provide you with a simple and seemless way of creating an exam.

  • Conduct final analysis and view performance.

Exam & Data Security

One of the things that could be going on in your mind, is exam cheating. You could be concerned that there will be cheating during exam due to lack of physical presence. I have thought deeply as well so that you do not have to worry about it. I have implemented browser monitoring strategies that detect each action being done on the browser. When cheating attempts are observed, the use is notified and when they persist, their exam is terminated.

Exam Analysis & Reports

Advanced analytics that provides in-depth, informative reports for each exam, section, candidate or performance. When an exam is done you can compare results of candidates, go through each question that was done and see how it was answered.

Live monitor as exams are being done

This is the icing on the cake. You will be able to have a live feed of everyone doing the exam. This is an added extra layer of security to tie all loose ends.
